# Lab 14: Digital Microphone (MP34DT05) - Sound Level Logging

## Objective

Capture samples from the onboard MP34DT05 microphone and log peak sound levels over Serial.

## Requirements

- Arduino Nano 33 BLE Sense and USB Micro-B cable
- Arduino IDE with the Nano 33 BLE board package (includes `PDM.h`)

## Files

- Sketch: [`code/sound_level_logging.ino`](code/sound_level_logging.ino)
- Evidence and recordings: [`images/`](images/) and [`videos/`](videos/)

## Run

1. Upload the sketch and open Serial Monitor or Serial Plotter.
2. Make sounds at different volumes near the microphone.
3. Observe the changing peak amplitude values.
